AI Provider Automation

When enabled, the extension will ask for permission to access AI sites so it can automatically paste the prompt. Without permission, the prompt is copied to your clipboard and the site is opened for you to paste manually.

Auto-Submit

When enabled, the extension will also click the send button after pasting.
